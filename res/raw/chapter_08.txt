%
Ashtavakra said --
It is bondage when the mind desires or grieves at anything, rejects or accepts
anything, feels happy or angry at anything.
%
Liberation is attained when the mind does not desire or grieve or reject or feel
happy or angry.
%
It is bondage when the mind is attached to any sense experience.
liberation when the mind is detached from all sense experiences.
%
When there is no ‘I,’ there is liberation; when there is ‘I,’ there is bondage.
Considering thus, easily refrain from accepting or rejecting anything.
